Education Outreach Comittee

The Educational Outreach Program focuses on preventative health through education. Committee members are dedicated UCSD students who are passionate about health and volunteer to promote healthy eating, exercise habits, and well-being to children from kindergarten to 5th grade. We plan lessons with focuses on educating children about mind, body, and nutrition and volunteer at the local Boys and Girls Club of Greater San Diego. We believe that setting a strong foundation of healthy habits early on will help children lead a healthy lifestyle in their future.
